About Epashikino Resort & Spa

A distinctive feature of Epashikino Resort is our spacious & tasteful furnished deluxe suites each with its own theme complementing the guest's mood of either business or leisure. The private balconies provide a breath taking, awe inspiring view of flamingoes foraging for food in the lake Elementaita with "sleeping Warrior" (a rocky mountain formation resembling a sleeping Moran) in the background. In the distance, steam from hot springs slowly rise above the horizon dancing in the rays of the tropical sun
